About

Hello, welcome to the home of the New York University Statistics Club. We’re part of the A3SR program and PRIISM. Feel free to reach out at stats.club@nyu.edu.

If you’re a first year student and have a specific question, feel free to contact whomever’s expertise best matches your question.
